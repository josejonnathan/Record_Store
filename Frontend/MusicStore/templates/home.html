Welcome to my music store management project!

I developed this project in different instances of front and backend.

In this platform, I have developed a robust backend that offers a variety of endpoints through our API. Access to the information in the album collection does not require authentication. Here, you can find an overview of the available albums.

However, for more advanced functions such as creating, modifying, and deleting entries, as well as accessing the full view of the collection, authentication is necessary. I have implemented authentication tokens to ensure the security and privacy of our users.

Additionally, the API integrates with the Deezer API, allowing me to complement our collection with additional information about the albums, such as covers, artists, and more.

In the frontend, I have used Django and Bootstrap to create an intuitive and appealing user experience. Here combine the information stored in the API with data provided by Deezer API, providing a complete experience for music lovers.

Thank you for visiting my project, I hope you enjoy exploring the music album collection!
